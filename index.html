About EverSudoku

EverSudoku is a modern take on the classic Sudoku puzzle game. Built with Flutter, it delivers a native experience across mobile, web, and desktop platforms.

Whether you're a Sudoku beginner or an experienced solver, EverSudoku provides the perfect environment to enjoy this timeless puzzle game. With intuitive controls, beautiful themes, and smart features, it's designed to make your Sudoku experience as enjoyable as possible.
